My Favourite

My personal favourite car

As you've probably come to realise, I really love cars, more specifically supercars. Then it shouldn't come as a surprise that my favourite supercar is a Bugatti Divo. One of the most beautiful and elegant supercars ever made. Besides the amazing silhouette of the car, it also packs some serious punch with it's W16 engine that produces more than 1400 hp. It has a 0-100 kph time of 2.4 seconds and a top speed of 236 mph/380 kph.
